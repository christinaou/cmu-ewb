Donate!

Our projects are in constant need of funding. Please consider donating to our organization. We receive some funding from EWB-USA and Carnegie Mellon University, this is not enough to fund our projects in their entirety. You can be assured that 100% of your donation will go towards funding our projects. Here's how you can donate:

Donate Through EWB-USA

Follow the link below to access Carnegie Mellon's donations page. To donate, click "View All Funds," select "Other," and specify "Engineers Without Borders" in the comments. You can then pay with your credit card and make a secure donation directly to our account with EWB-USA.
